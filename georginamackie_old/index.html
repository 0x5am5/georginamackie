Georgina's passion and specialism is outdoor training with her clients. Her sessions are perfectly suited for weight loss, strength training and lifelong holistic fitness.

"I can't understand people slugging it out for hours in an overcrowded, expensive gym when you can be in the great outdoors breathing in fresh air?"

Research has proven that undertaking exercise outdoors provides a much greater feeling of wellbeing which can lead to rapid and sustainable results. Boost your energy levels, exercise enjoyment and engagement. It can also help prevent depression and tension!
Georgina is a qualified personal trainer (REPS Level 4). Her aim is to make exercise fun and enjoyable.

"I believe health and fitness is a lifestyle. However it's all about balance. That slice of cake (organic of course!!) after a massive run is totally deserved!!""
