Sports Massage

Georgina is a fully qualified Sports Massage Therapist

Sports massage therapy will help alleviate the stress and tension, which builds up in the body's soft tissues during physical activity.
Massage will help to drain away fatigue, to relieve swelling, to reduce muscle tension, to promote flexibility, to prevent injuries and, in the case of sports people, prepare for peak performance. Sports massage can help prevent those niggling injuries that so often get in the way of performance and achievement, whether a person is an athlete, a once a week jogger or just physically active.

Nutritional advice

We are constantly being bombarded with fad diets and false promises. Every magazine you pick up promises to give you the body you've always dreamed of. The consequence of this conflicting advice can even result in weight gain rather than loss!
It’s so confusing to know which way to turn if you're trying to lose weight, improve your shape and develop sustainable habits for healthy eating.
The truth is we're all totally different, with individual body types and strengths and weaknesses. There's no "one size fits all" approach or miracle diet when it comes to planning nutrition.

Georgina will adapt your diet to suit your goals and lifestyle. She will create a bespoke nutrition plan and menu ideas so you don't get stuck for inspiration.
